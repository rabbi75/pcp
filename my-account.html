In addition to uploading and downloading your images through our web-based customer portal, you can also choose the option to use BrickFTP.

BrickFTP is the secure SFTP hosting server we use to transfer image files. We love it because it supports 5TB file uploads on all interfaces and works with popular programs like FileZilla.
